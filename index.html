OUR STORY

Founded back in 2025, Infinity Roleplay started as a small community of passionate GTA V players who wanted to create a more immersive and realistic roleplay experience. What began as a group of 10 dedicated roleplayers has now grown into one of the most respected RP communities.

Our team of developers, administrators, and moderators work tirelessly to create unique scripts, custom vehicles, and immersive gameplay elements that you won't find on other servers. We pride ourselves on our attention to detail and commitment to providing the best possible roleplay experience.

At Infinity Roleplay, we believe in the power of community-driven development. Many of our best features and updates have come directly from player suggestions. We regularly host community events, competitions, and gatherings to strengthen our bonds and create memorable experiences for everyone.

Whether you're interested in law enforcement, criminal enterprises, business ownership, or just living a civilian life in our vibrant city, Infinity Roleplay offers endless possibilities for creating your own unique story in Los Santos.

General Server Rules

1

Respect All Players

Treat all players with respect regardless of their role, experience level, or background. Discrimination, harassment, or bullying of any kind will not be tolerated.

2

No Cheating or Exploiting

Use of cheats, hacks, exploits, or any third-party tools to gain an unfair advantage will result in a permanent ban without warning.

3

Staff Decisions Are Final

Respect our decisions even when harsh. If you disagree with a decision, you may appeal through the proper channels on our Discord.

4

One Character Per Person

You may create multiple characters, but only one character can be active at a time. Character switching to avoid RP consequences is prohibited.

5

Appropriate Character Names

Character names must be realistic and appropriate. No offensive, celebrity, or meme names are allowed.

6

No Combat Logging

Disconnecting to avoid roleplay situations, especially during combat or police interactions, is prohibited.

7

Report Bugs

If you discover a bug or exploit, report it to staff immediately. Abuse of bugs for personal gain is prohibited.

Roleplay Guidelines

1

Value Your Life

Always act as if your character values their life. Don't make unrealistic decisions that put your character in unnecessary danger.

2

No Metagaming

Do not use out-of-character information to influence in-character actions.

3

No Powergaming

Do not force actions on other players without giving them a chance to react.

4

Stay In Character

Remain in character during roleplay scenes. Use OOC chat only when necessary.

5

No Breaking Character

Stay in character at all times during roleplay interactions. Use appropriate channels for OOC communication.

Police & Criminal Rules

1

Police Response

Police must respond appropriately to the level of crime. Not every situation requires maximum force.

2

Criminal Activity

All criminal activities must have proper roleplay and planning. Random violence without roleplay context is prohibited.

3

Robbery Cooldowns

Respect the cooldown periods between robberies and other major criminal activities.

4

Gang Activity

Gang wars and territory disputes must be approved by staff and have proper roleplay context.

Vehicles & Properties Rules

1

Vehicle Usage

Use vehicles appropriately for their intended purpose. Don't use off-road vehicles for racing or sports cars for off-roading.

2

Property Ownership

Respect other players' properties. Breaking and entering requires proper roleplay context.

3

Vehicle Theft

Vehicle theft must have roleplay context. Random vehicle theft without interaction is prohibited.

Communication Rules

1

Voice Chat

Use proximity voice chat appropriately. No shouting, playing music, or using voice changers without roleplay context.

2

Radio Communication

Use radio channels appropriately for their intended purpose. Emergency services have priority on their channels.

3

Text Chat

Keep text chat in character. Use appropriate channels for OOC communication.

Rule Enforcement

Rules are enforced via a strike system. Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent bans from the server.

If you have questions about any rules or need clarification, please contact our staff team on Discord.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What are the requirements to apply?

Requirements vary by position, but generally you need to be an active member of our community, have a good understanding of roleplay principles, and meet the minimum age requirement of 16 years.

How long does the application process take?

The application review process typically takes 3-7 days, depending on the volume of applications and the position you're applying for.

Can I apply for multiple positions?

Yes, you can apply for multiple positions, but we recommend focusing on one role at a time to increase your chances of success.

What happens if my application is rejected?

If your application is rejected, you'll receive feedback on areas for improvement. You can reapply after 30 days with a new application addressing those areas.
